Get your panel data from the Enphase Gateway

Your Enphase IQ Gateway has a local API that reports per-panel production data. Open it in your browser, copy the data, and paste it here.

1
Open your gateway: https://YOUR_GATEWAY_IP
Find the IP in your router or Enphase app (System → Network). Accept the certificate warning — this is normal.
2
Log in. If it asks for a token:
  1. Go to entrez.enphaseenergy.com/entrez_tokens
  2. Log in with your Enphase account
  3. Enter your Gateway serial number
  4. Select owner → click Generate
  5. Copy the token, paste it on the gateway login, click Submit

Tokens last ~12 months.

3
Once logged in, go to:
https://YOUR_GATEWAY_IP/api/v1/production/inverters
You'll see JSON data — text with brackets and numbers.
4
Select all (Ctrl+A), copy (Ctrl+C), paste below.
Contains only serial numbers and wattage — no personal info.
